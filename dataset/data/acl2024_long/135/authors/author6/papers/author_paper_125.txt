    "title": "THUNLP at TAC KBP 2013 in Entity Linking",
    "abstract": "This paper describes the THUNLP system submitted to Entity Linking task of KBP Track in TAC 2013. This system achieves the third place with a B-cubed+ F1 score of 0.712 in mono-lingual entity linking task. In Chinese cross-lingual entity linking, this system achieved B-cubed+ F1 score of 0.647 with the help of Google Translate."
