This bake-off task of Chinese word segmentation is focused on the perfor- mance of Chinese word segmentation al- gorithms on MicroBlog corpora. 17 groups submitted 20 results, among which the best system has all the P, R and F values near 95%, and the average values of the 17 systems are 0.8931, 0.8981 and 0.8953, respectively."
